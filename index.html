Bio

Tara Benitez has passionately pursued her yoga journey for the last 15 years. After receiving her 200-hour Vinyasa Yoga certification through the Joschi Yoga Institute, a Yoga Alliance Registered school, she continued her studies with an advanced certificate in Anatomy and Movement from Leslie Kaminoff’s Yoga Anatomy program. She has been an avid meditator since 2007 and completed two ten-day Vipassana silent retreats in Massachusetts and Thailand.

Her experience in a variety of martial arts, running, biking, climbing and meditation has helped her create a conscious, alignment-oriented, vinyasa yoga flow.


During the day, Tara is a NYC Department of Education, Special Education Teacher to children on the autism spectrum. She has taught students from kindergarten to 12th grades. Tara's master's degree is in Urban Special Education from Long Island University, Brooklyn.

For those students who want 1 on 1 instruction: Tara is available for individualized yoga practices, meditation, small groups, private parties for children and adults.
